三毛 Sanmao

撒哈拉沙漠的傳奇作家
Legendary Writers of the Sahara Desert

生平 Life

1943年，三毛誕生。父母親依據祖譜上的排序，為她取名「陳懋平」。因為學不會寫「懋」字，便自己改名為「陳平」。多年後，又為自己取了一個筆名「三毛」，原因是她戲謔地認為自己「只值三毛錢」。

In 1943, Sanmao was born. Her parents named her "Chen Maoping" according to the order in the family genealogy. As she couldn't write the character "懋" (mao), she changed her name to "Chen Ping." Many years later, she adopted the pen name "Sanmao" for herself, jokingly believing that she was "only worth three cents."

1974年，三毛與戀人荷西在撒哈拉沙漠完婚，並以其在撒哈拉沙漠的生活及見聞為背景，發表充滿異國風情的散文作品《撒哈拉故事》，因而成名。她讓華語世界吹起了一股「三毛熱」，也將「流浪文學」推至巔峰。代表著作有《撒哈拉的故事》、《我的快樂天堂》、《哭泣的駱駝》……等。

In 1974, Sanmao and her lover José got married in the Sahara Desert. Inspired by their life and experiences in the desert, she published the exotic prose collection "Stories of the Sahara," which brought her fame. She sparked a "Sanmao craze" in the Chinese-speaking world and elevated "wandering literature" to its peak. Her notable works include "Stories of the Sahara," "My Happy Paradise," "The Crying Camel," and more.

1991年，三毛被發現在台北榮總病房內逝世，結束她絢爛的一生。

In 1991, Sanmao was found to have passed away in the ward of Taipei Veterans General Hospital, bringing an end to her illustrious life.
